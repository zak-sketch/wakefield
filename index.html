Who We Are

Wakefield Print and Signs is a proudly Kenyan-owned creative agency based in Kitengela. We specialize in graphic design, printing, branding, large-format printing, and signage solutions — helping businesses bring their vision to life through bold, impactful visuals.

What We Do

We transform your ideas into powerful visual assets. Our core services include:

  • Graphic Design – Logos, marketing collateral, custom creatives
  • Digital Printing – Flyers, posters, business cards, packaging & more
  • Large Format Printing – Banners, billboards, backdrops & vehicle branding
  • Branding – Corporate wear, promotional items, merchandise
  • Signage – 3D signs, shopfront branding, wayfinding signs, vehicle wraps
