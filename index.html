Who We Are

Wakefield Print and Signs is a proudly Kenyan-owned creative agency based in Kitengela. We specialize in graphic design, printing, branding, large-format printing, and signage solutions — helping businesses bring their vision to life through bold, impactful visuals.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the market leader in creative branding and signage across Africa, delivering high-impact solutions that elevate our clients' visibility and growth.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, innovative, and affordable branding solutions that help businesses stand out in a competitive market.

What We Do

We transform your ideas into powerful visual assets. Our core services include:

Graphic Design

Logos, marketing collateral, custom creatives tailored to your brand identity.

Digital Printing

Flyers, posters, business cards, packaging & more with exceptional quality.

Large Format Printing

Banners, billboards, backdrops & vehicle branding using our 3.2-meter wide printer.

Branding

Corporate wear, promotional items, merchandise to strengthen your brand presence.

Signage

3D signs, shopfront branding, wayfinding signs, vehicle wraps for maximum visibility.

Industries We Serve

Retail, real estate, events, hospitality, education, health, and more.
