About Wakefield Print & Signs

Wakefield is your trusted partner in printing, branding, and signage solutions. Based in Kitengela, Kenya, we bring your vision to life through creative design, cutting-edge technology, and outstanding service.

As a full-service signage company, we handle every step from on-site field surveys to professional fabrication and expert installation. Let us handle the complexity—you focus on your brand.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality, and innovative branding solutions that give our clients a competitive edge in any market.

Our Vision

To become the leading brand in creative signage and visual branding across Africa, empowering businesses to be seen and remembered.
